Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

A hearty Italian pasta dish with a rich, savory meat sauce. This comfort food classic is perfect for family dinners and can be made in under an hour!

Recipe Information

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 4 people

Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g ground beef
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and garlic; saut√© until softened and fragrant.
  3. Stir in the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Mix in tomato paste, canned tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  6. Drain spaghetti and serve topped with the Bolognese sauce.
  7. Garnish with fresh basil and grated parmesan if desired.

Tips & Notes

For a richer flavor, let the sauce simmer longer. You can also substitute ground beef with ground turkey for a lighter option.
